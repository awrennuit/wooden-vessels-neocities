Welcome

Wooden Vessels is a dungeon synth project led by Aveline and based in Minnesota, USA. Wooden Vessels is part of the Great Lakes Dungeon Synth collective.

Don't miss the first Great Lakes Dungeon Siege in Fort Wayne, IN in October! You can also watch it on Twitch.

Aveline's Cave Bird Records also hosts three other projects. An atmospheric black metal project called awren, a funeral doom project called Gloomthread, and an ambient guitar project called chrysanthemum.
